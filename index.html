Ableton

We make Live, Push and Link — unique software and hardware for music creation and performance.

Ableton was released the first version of Live in 2001. Our products are used by musicians, sound designers, and artists.

We’re really proud of the work we’ve done so far. But there’s so much more to come. If you’d like to be a part of it, please join us.

We want our employees to love it here.we’re looking for exceptional talent from around the world.

Office hours are flexible, but it’s not all work; we have several company and team outings throughout the year as well as a variety of fun, informal small-group activities.

Making music isn’t easy. It takes time, effort, and learning. But when you’re in the flow, it’s incredibly rewarding.

We feel the same way about making Ableton products. The driving force behind Ableton is our passion for what we make, and the people we make it for.

Apprenticeships

What are they and what do you need to get started?

Dual apprenticeship programs have a long tradition in Germany, and they play an important role in the country’s economy. The programs are a proven stepping stone towards a meaningful career without the need for a university degree. They give you the chance to apply theoretical knowledge learned in a vocational school to tasks in the workplace, while you divide your time between class and office days.
